Poquito Burritos

Poquito Burritos is a Mexican food business headquartered in Indianapolis, Indiana. Our business was founded in 2021 by Brady Schmidt, Jaiden Patel, Madeline Wachnicki, Aiden O'Brien and Evan Carmona. We had a dream of putting a twist on traditional Mexican cuisine, and making sure all of Indiana could get a taste. Our unique business proposition is that we sell miniature Mexican food, which is where our name "Poquito Burritos" comes from. It means "Small Burritos" in Spanish.

Our company sells various Mexican items such as the following:

  • Quesadillas
  • Burritos
  • Bowls
  • Tacos
However, our most famous delicacy is the world-renowned poquito churro. We mainly reach our buyers from a DTC approach that allows us to interact with our consumers face-to-face and build valuable connections. We mainly carry this out through our central location - The Garage Food Hall, in Indianapolis. Along with our standstill location, we have 5 food trucks that scatter across Indiana daily to various cities and universities and get our product out to the public. The 5 most popular cities our food trucks visit are:
  1. Indianapolis
  2. Fishers
  3. Carmel
  4. Bloomington
  5. West Lafayette
